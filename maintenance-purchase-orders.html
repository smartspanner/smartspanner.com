Control inventory levels, react to maintenance failures and control your expenditure with built-in purchasing module

The purchasing module in Smartspanner allows you to raise purchase orders directly from spare part records or when stock levels fall below minimum thresholds. Track your spending by supplier, site, and individual spare parts.

Goods Receipt Notes (GRN) ensure proper tracking of received items and automatic inventory updates, keeping your stock levels accurate and up-to-date.
